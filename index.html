About

Who I am…

A personable, degreed, IT professional with secret clearance, adept at bridging the gap between business and technical staff. I have worked extensively with organizational stakeholders from executive level to entry-level clerks to discover the vision and the business needs of clients and turn them into functional specifications, logical and physical data models, and finally implementing the new system and processes.

What I do…

  • Discover the vision of the client by facilitating JAD sessions and working with them individually to understand how their vision fits with the organization’s goals.
  • Design practical plans on how to achieve the optimal solution, including creating a functional specification, developing the solution architecture and design, and preparing resource estimates.
  • Deploy a system that successfully meets the organizations business needs.

How I add value…

  • Quickly establish positive relationships with stakeholders.
  • Proactively seek resolutions for complex problems.
  • Create detailed and accurate requirements specifications with associated artifacts as required, communicating with the stakeholders the look and feel as well as the specifics of how the system will work.
  • Evaluate current workflows, design future workflows and create the work breakdown structure to manage the project.
  • Quick study, learning new environments, systems, tools and technologies quickly.
